# Thank you for joining us at SRCCON 2026! {#ticket}

We seriously appreciate you for taking the time to complete our entry and ticketing process for SRCCON 2026! The input you offer and the ways you choose to participate at SRCCON make it the special event it is. We can't wait for you to join us! 

If you have a friend, peer or colleague who might need SRCCON in their lives, feel free to let them know how they can [take part](/attendees) and share the [call for participation form](/participation/form/) so they can let us know they're interested. We'll keep the CFP open for attendees as long as space remains available.!

Please feel free to reach out to us at [srccon@opennews.org](mailto:srccon@opennews.org) with any questions. We'll be in touch soon!
